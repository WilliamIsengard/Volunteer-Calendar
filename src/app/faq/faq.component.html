FAQs

Please review the information below for valuable details about The Volunteer Calendar


Do you have to pay to use The Volunteer Calendar?

The Volunteer Calendar is completely free to use. You are welcome to support your local nonprofits by donating via our donate page. 100% of all donations go to the nonprofit of your choice.


Why was The Volunteer Calendar created?

The Volunteer Calendar was created to provide an easy way to empower folks to support nonprofits in their local community. We aim to remove barriers for volunteering to expand community involvement and better the world.


How can I volunteer?

The Volunteer Calendar provides two simple ways to find volunteer opportunities:
1) Visit our Calendar page to find upcoming events to join that works within your schedule.
2) Visit our Categories page to find nonprofits that support things you care about (ex. Animals) and see events specific to those organizations.


Do I need an account to use The Volunteer Calendar?

You do not need to create an account to view events or nonprofit details, but it will allow you to join events to receive updates and have a profile page with your scheduled events and favorited nonprofits.
